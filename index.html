Hi, there!

These WordPress developer tests will help us evaluate candidates’ abilities in WordPress customization and plugin development.

The purpose of this exercise is for us to get a sense of how you would approach designing and implementing a simple WordPress integration before we get you in for the next stage process interview. We’re avoiding tricky algorithmic tests in favor of something that shows how you approach problems and organise a codebase.

Candidates who do well on one on these test can leverage WordPress to develop fast, secure, and stable WordPress websites.
